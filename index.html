🚀 Getting Started — How to Use Energy Trade Lab
🔒 Personal or Public?
Right now it's 100% personal — all your data (checklist, journal, portfolio, trades) lives in your browser's localStorage only. Nobody else can see it.

To make it public (host for others): Upload to GitHub Pages (free). Anyone with the link can use their own private copy — their data stays in their own browser too. It's like giving everyone their own blank workbook.

To share P&L publicly: Use the Community tab → Share Your Performance. That optionally submits your stats to the leaderboard via Formspree (requires a free Formspree account setup).
📅 Daily Workflow (5–10 mins)
1. Open app → check the TradingView live ticker at top for today's oil/stock prices
2. Go to 📈 Price Monitor → copy today's prices into the input cards → click Generate Analysis for S/R levels, stops, probability
3. Go to ✅ 14-Step Checklist → work through Steps 1–9 at minimum (macro, news, war score, conviction)
4. Back on Dashboard → select SOD (Start of Day) → click ⚡ EXECUTE CHECKLIST → get your daily recommendation table
5. If entering a trade → go to 💼 Portfolio → Add Position with entry price, shares, stop, target
6. End of day: select EOD → Execute Checklist again → journal entry auto-saved
🤖 How to Initiate AI Paper Trading
The AI Trade Engine reads your checklist inputs to make decisions — so the richer your inputs, the smarter it trades.

Step 1: Fill in Step 7 of the Checklist with today's prices for all 7 stocks (or use Price Monitor → they sync)
Step 2: Set Step 4 escalation score (0–100 war risk slider) and sentiment
Step 3: Set Step 9 conviction scores per stock (sliders)
Step 4: Go to 🤖 AI Trades tab → click "Run AI Analysis"

The AI scores every stock 0–100 using all inputs, decides BUY/ACCUMULATE/HOLD/AVOID, calculates position sizes, and auto-enters paper positions (when price is available). Every run is logged to the AI Journal automatically.
🧮 Why Does the Allocation Tab Show 65%?
The allocation sliders mirror your Step 9 conviction scores from the Checklist. The default is 65% because you haven't set them yet — it's a neutral starting point.

To fix it: Go to ✅ Checklist → Step 9 → move the sliders for each stock to your actual conviction level → the Allocation tab will automatically reflect those values.

What the allocation calculation does: 80%+ conviction = full $5,000 allocation · 60–79% = $3,000 · 40–59% = $1,500 · Below 40% = $0. Total is capped at $20,000.
📓 Understanding the Journal Lab
The Journal has 3 separate spaces:

☀️ Daily Posts — Auto-created every time you Execute Checklist (SOD or EOD). You never have to write these — the system generates them from your inputs. Shows conviction, Brent price, war risk, top picks, portfolio value.

📆 Weekly Reflections — You write these every Friday. What worked, what didn't, key lessons. Saves to your browser permanently. When Formspree is set up, you can email it to yourself.

📅 Monthly Learnings — End-of-month strategy review. Best/worst trade, rule improvements. Your trading evolution diary.

Email delivery: Set up Formspree (free, 5 min) → Community tab → Setup Guide. Once connected, your weekly/monthly entries can be emailed to yourself automatically.
